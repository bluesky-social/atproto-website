OAuth scopes are how you request permission to create, update, and delete records from a repo, call XRPC methods, or perform certain account management functions. Individual scopes tend to correspond to specific Lexicons, XRPC methods, or management actions, and many scopes can be bundled together into permission sets, while some scopes must be requested individually.

This tool lets you interactively select the scopes your app might want to request, such as uploading images or videos to a person's PDS (the `blob:*/*` scope), the full chat client permission set (`include:chat.bsky.authFullChatClient`) if you are building a fully featured Bluesky client, as well as permission sets from a few other apps.
