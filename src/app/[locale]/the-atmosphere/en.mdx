The Atmosphere is a new open network built on the AT Protocol.

## Your account, everywhere

Your account works across all Atmosphere apps. Sign in once and bring your identity, your connections, and your content with you wherever you go.

## Own your relationships

Build on your own social graph and implement your own algorithms. The Atmosphere gives you the freedom to connect with others on your own terms, so you don't need to chase platform changes.

## Create your own apps

Anyone can create new apps and experiences that plug into the same open network. Never start from zero.

## Web-native, for real

The Atmosphere connects naturally with personal websites and the broader web. Your handle can be your own domain.

## Bluesky is an Atmosphere app

[Bluesky](https://bsky.app) is the first app built on the Atmosphere. See [more](https://bsky.social/about).
