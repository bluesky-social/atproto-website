Jim and Alex are back after a holiday weekend and a backlog of atproto news. Daniel Holmgren has shipped the permissioned data proposal just in time to celebrate America's semiquincentennial, with the help of plenty of community input and a new communities feature in the Bluesky app being developed in lockstep.

Tangled has added full-text code search and a new microVM-back CI engine, and there's a new tool for mirroring your GitHub repos over there. Plus, new releases from Anisota and the general availability of Roomy.

We also take a digression or two into effective outreach on other platforms, and don't miss the new job posting on the Bluesky Atmosphere team.
