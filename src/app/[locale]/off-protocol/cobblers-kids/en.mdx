Jim and Alex return to the livestream, now at a new time and day and even a scheduling widget on the [Off Protocol homepage](https://atproto.com/off-protocol) built on the [event community lexicon](https://lexicon.garden/lexicon/did:plc:2uwoih2htodskvgocarwv5eq/community.lexicon.calendar.event).
