Head of product at ROOST, Juliet Shen, drops by for a conversation about Coop reaching version 1.0. Coop is ROOST's review and moderation platform used by organizations of all shapes and sizes, including Bluesky. It's also the first trust and safety tool that manages detection, review, and reporting in a free and open source tool.

Juliet and Alex get into why encoding moderation policy in the open matters—including [The Christchurch Call Foundation](https://www.christchurchcall.org/)—and where AI actually fits into a moderation workflow.

They also dig into what the 1.0 release means in practice: a more manageable memory footprint, Scylla now optional (with some tentpole summer movie cross-promo potential), and the ability to run a full production instance on a small team's budget.
